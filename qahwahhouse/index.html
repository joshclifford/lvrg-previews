Our Heritage

Eight Generations of Coffee Mastery

From the ancient highlands of Yemen to your cup, Qahwah House carries forward a legacy spanning eight generations of coffee cultivation. Our beans are sourced directly from Al Hasbani Farms, where our family has perfected the art of growing premium Arabica coffee.

Every sip tells a story of tradition, craftsmanship, and an unwavering commitment to quality that has been passed down through centuries.
