The OFF-PEAK Experience

Where Innovation Meets Tradition

At OFF-PEAK COFFEE, we believe great coffee shouldn't be rushed. Our sleek, modern spaces in Flushing invite you to pause and savor the ritual of coffee — a refined experience meant to be enjoyed at your own pace.

From careful bean selection to advanced brewing technology, we ensure consistent quality and exceptional flavor in every cup. Whether you're seeking a classic Americano or our signature Black Sesame Latte, each drink is crafted with precision and care.
