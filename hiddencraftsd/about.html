// FROM THE TAPS

We opened Hidden Craft because we were tired of two things: waiting 15 minutes for a bartender to pour a beer we already knew we wanted, and committing to a full pint of something we'd never tried before. Neither of those felt right for a city that takes craft beer as seriously as San Diego.

So we built the room we wanted to drink in. 30+ self-serve taps of the best local breweries pouring right now. A card that lets you try an ounce, a six, or a full pour. You walk the wall, you pour what you want, you sit down. No gatekeepers.

Then we put a real scratch kitchen behind it because great beer deserves great food — not fried everything. Tacos, bowls, burgers, seasonal specials that shift when the farmers have something interesting. And a patio for the dogs, because this is still San Diego.

We're at India & C Street. We do private events for local teams, we cater offices across downtown, and we throw live music on weekends because the neighborhood deserves a third place that isn't a chain. That's the whole pitch.

What We Believe

Three rules. No exceptions.

01

Local or Nothing

Every tap is a San Diego brewery. No imports, no corporate macro-lagers. We rotate often so there's always something new to pour.

02

Honest Pricing

Pay by the ounce. Try five beers for the price of one pint. No bottle-service theatrics, no markup tricks. Just good beer, priced fairly.

03

Dogs Welcome

It's the rule that tells you who we are. If your dog is good, the patio is open. Water bowls provided. Biscuits negotiable.
