Our Story

More Than Just a Coffee Shop

At Black Sheep Coffee House, we are a refreshing beacon in your day—a place to savor and enjoy life's simple pleasures. Nestled in the heart of Queens at 101-20 Astoria Blvd, we bring a unique touch to New York's vibrant coffee scene.

Every cup we serve is crafted with intention, using only the finest organic, dark roast beans. We believe great coffee brings people together, and that's exactly what we're here for.
