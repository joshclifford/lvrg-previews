Our Story

Fifteen years of cooking for strangers who became friends.

We don't deliver trays. We bring a kitchen.

I started Toast with one rule — if I wouldn't serve it to my own family at my own kitchen table, it doesn't go out the door. Fifteen years later, that's still the only rule. Everything else we've figured out along the way.

We're not a banquet hall with a delivery van. We're a real chef team, and we show up to your venue with the full kitchen — burners, prep tables, the whole thing. Your guests hear the sear. They smell the garlic hitting the pan. By the time the food lands in front of them, they're already hungry.

San Diego's been good to us. We've catered weddings in Balboa Park, corporate dinners in La Jolla, bar mitzvahs in Coronado, and a surprising number of backyard milestones in North Park. Every single one taught us something. Every single one still matters.

The team's grown. The menu's grown. But what hasn't changed is this: when we say yes to your event, we mean it. We're cooking, we're there, and we're invested in your day going exactly the way you've been picturing it.

— The Toast Catering Team
