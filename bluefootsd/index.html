About Bluefoot

WHERE NORTH PARK
COMES TO PLAY

Nestled at the iconic corner of 30th and Upas, Bluefoot Bar + Lounge has become the heartbeat of North Park's nightlife scene. We're not just a bar—we're a destination for those who appreciate quality drinks, incredible music, and genuine connections.

From early morning soccer watch parties to late-night DJ sets, we've created a space where every hour feels like happy hour and every guest becomes family.
