WHERE GOOD PEOPLE GATHER

Nestled in the heart of North Park at the corner of 30th and Upas, Bluefoot Bar + Lounge has become the neighborhood's go-to spot for great drinks, live DJ sets, and that unmistakable community vibe that keeps locals coming back night after night.

Whether you're catching an early soccer match or dancing until 2am on the weekend, our doors are always open with happy hour pricing all day long. Because here at Bluefoot, every hour is happy hour.
