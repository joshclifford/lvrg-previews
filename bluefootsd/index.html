About The Foot

YOUR NORTH PARK
NEIGHBORHOOD SPOT

Bluefoot Bar + Lounge sits right at the corner of 30th and Upas in the heart of North Park. We're not trying to be fancy — just a laid-back spot where you can grab drinks, catch some beats from our live DJs, and hang with good people.

Whether you're starting your day early for soccer or rolling in late on a weekend, we've got you covered. Open by 11am (earlier for games) and going strong til 2am on weekends.
