Our Story

YOUR NEIGHBORHOOD SPOT

Bluefoot Bar + Lounge isn't just another bar—it's where North Park comes together. Located at the corner of 30th and Upas, we've created a welcoming space where everyone's a regular.

Whether you're catching the early morning soccer match, unwinding after work, or dancing til 2am on the weekend, we're here for it. No pretense, no velvet ropes—just good drinks, great music, and dang good people.
