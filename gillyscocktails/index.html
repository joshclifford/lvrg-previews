What We Offer

Your Home Away From Home

Well-Crafted Cocktails

Our skilled bartenders mix up classic favorites and creative house specials. Every drink is made with care and quality ingredients—just the way it should be.

Ice Cold Beers

From local San Diego craft brews to your favorite domestic classics, we keep the taps flowing and the glasses frosty. There's always something good on draft.

Pool Tables & Games

Challenge your friends to a friendly game of pool. Our tables are always ready for some competition—whether you're a shark or just looking for fun.
