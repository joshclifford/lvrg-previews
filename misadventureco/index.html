The Story

Made from the bread nobody got to eat.

Our vodka starts where most stories end — with day-old baked goods destined for landfill. We rescue them, ferment them, distill them, and bottle them. The result: 18 spirits awards, a lighter footprint, and a vodka that tastes like the risk paid off.
