The Experience

A Journey Through
Taisho Roman Era

Hidden behind a secret door at Asa Cafe and Bakery, Bar Kamon transports you to the romantic elegance of 1920s Japan. Our speakeasy features wood-paneled walls, vintage furnishings, and crystal chandeliers that create an atmosphere of sophisticated mystery.

Every cocktail is a masterpiece—from our Clarified Tokyo Banana to Hojicha-Infused Negronis—crafted exclusively with Japanese-made spirits by bartenders with up to 15 years of experience.
