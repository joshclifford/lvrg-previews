About Bar Kamon

A Secret Worth Discovering

Hidden behind a secret door at Asa Cafe and Bakery in San Diego's East Village, Bar Kamon transports you to the elegance of 1920s Japan. Wood-paneled walls, vintage furnishings, and crystal chandeliers create the perfect backdrop for an unforgettable evening.

Our expert bartenders, each with years of experience, craft innovative cocktails like the Clarified Tokyo Banana and Hojicha-Infused Negroni, paired with authentic Japanese cuisine.
