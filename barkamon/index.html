Our Story

A Portal to
1920s Japan

Hidden behind a secret door at Asa Cafe and Bakery in San Diego's East Village, Bar Kamon transports you to the romantic Taisho era of Japan. Step inside and discover wood-paneled walls, vintage furnishings, and crystal chandeliers that evoke an era of elegance and sophistication.

Our master bartenders craft innovative Japanese cocktails like the Clarified Tokyo Banana and Hojicha-Infused Negronis, paired with authentic Japanese culinary fare including chicken katsu sandwiches and takoyaki.
