A Hidden Sanctuary of
Refined Indulgence

Bar Kamon is a 1920s-inspired Taisho Roman cocktail lounge hidden behind a secret door at Asa Cafe and Bakery in downtown San Diego's East Village.

Experience a sophisticated atmosphere with wood-paneled walls, vintage furnishings, and crystal chandeliers while savoring innovative drinks like the Clarified Tokyo Banana and Hojicha-Infused Negronis.
