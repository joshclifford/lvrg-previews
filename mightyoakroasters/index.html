Our Story

Why We Roast
With Wood Fire

The first reason is flavor. The heat created by a wood fire comes into direct contact with the coffee, imparting subtle aromatic flavors of the particular wood used—without the smokey taste you might expect.

The second is sustainability. By sourcing wood from local arborists within a 50-mile radius, we're giving new purpose to trees that would otherwise be discarded—completing a cycle of environmental responsibility from origin to cup.
