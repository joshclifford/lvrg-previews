Our Story

A Taste of Italy in San Diego

Caffè Calabria brings the warmth and tradition of Italian coffee culture to America's Finest City. Our beans are sourced directly from Calabria, roasted with care, and brewed with passion.

Every visit is an invitation to slow down, savor the moment, and experience coffee the way it was meant to be enjoyed — with friends, family, and community.
