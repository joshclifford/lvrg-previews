Our Story

Turning Waste Into Wonder

Every year, billions of pounds of perfectly good baked goods are thrown away. We saw a problem—and an opportunity. By rescuing surplus bread, pastries, and baked goods from local bakeries, we've created the world's first carbon-negative vodka.

Our award-winning spirit isn't just about great taste—it's about rewriting the rules of sustainable spirits. Each bottle represents rescued ingredients, reduced emissions, and a commitment to proving that doing good and tasting great aren't mutually exclusive.
