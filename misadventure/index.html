Our Story

Turning Food Waste Into Liquid Gold

Every year, billions of pounds of perfectly good baked goods are thrown away. We saw an opportunity to create something extraordinary from what others discard.

Our Vista distillery transforms surplus bread, pastries, and baked goods into smooth, award-winning vodka—proving that sustainability and premium quality go hand in hand.

✓ Small Batch Crafted
✓ Locally Sourced

Environmental Impact

Each bottle of Misadventure Vodka prevents food waste and removes more carbon from the atmosphere than it creates. Cheers to that.
